About plastic Neutrality

What is it?

Plastic Neutrality is the responsible usage and recycle of plastic to balance out our impact on the environment

Plastic Credit

A plastic credit is a certified representing one kg of plastic waste recovered & recycled from the environment.

Footprinting

We assess the quality of plastic that a brand annually generates.Indivuals can calculate their plastic footprint using our calculator.

Recovery & Recycling

Brands can then fund the removal of same amount of plastic waste from nature as its annual footprint.

Reduction & Redesign

Colloborate with us to minimise plastic usage and explore alternatives.
